好 Hǎo
AI-Assisted E-Book · 5 Dishes · Pika Animation · 2025
An e-book reimagining five iconic Taiwanese dishes through the lens of artificial intelligence — probing the dilemma and inevitability of AI in food culture and creative practice. Hand-drawn sketches of each dish were fed into Pika to generate 5-second animations, collapsing the boundary between human craft and machine vision. The tension between authenticity and generation sits at the heart of every page.
