Exhibition & Performance

Performance art

NYU Shanghai Photo Exhibition
Two Works · 2025
I — I Am the Typhoon

A self-portrait series reimagining the body as a typhoon — tracing the eye of the storm inward, where stillness and destruction coexist. Light, exposure, and physical gesture collapse the boundary between natural force and personal identity.

II — The Last Hour of Death Row

A performance piece confronting the final hour before execution — exploring how time, memory, and the body behave under the weight of finality. A meditative tension between resistance and surrender.

Off-Campus Exhibition · Action and Gesture
Eyes on Me
Durational Performance · Body as Archive

A durational performance in which eyes collected from the artist's childhood photographs — each representing a different age and stage of life — are placed across the body. The work unfolds as a repeated ritual: selecting, arranging, and ultimately attempting to remove them. Three emotional registers — joy, grief, and neutrality — guide each cycle, until the final act of erasure.
